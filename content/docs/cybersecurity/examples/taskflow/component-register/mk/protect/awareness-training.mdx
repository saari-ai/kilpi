### PR.AT-01: General awareness

| Topic | Audience | Delivery | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| CMS usage and content approval workflow | CMS editors | Onboarding walkthrough | Onboarding + as needed |
| Secret handling (API keys never in code or logs) | Frontend team | Onboarding briefing + CI enforcement | Onboarding + continuous via CI |
| Supply chain awareness (dependency risks) | Frontend team | Team session | Annually |
