ISO 27001 is the international standard for information security management systems (ISMS). Annex A provides a catalogue of 93 controls across four domains. These controls are the primary selection pool for our security programme.

## Annex A domains

| Domain | Scope | Control count |
|---|---|---|
| A.5 | Organisational controls — policies, roles, responsibilities, threat intelligence, supplier relationships | 37 |
| A.6 | People controls — screening, awareness, training, disciplinary processes | 8 |
| A.7 | Physical controls — perimeters, entry, equipment protection | 14 |
| A.8 | Technological controls — access, cryptography, network security, secure development, configuration | 34 |

Not all 93 controls are relevant to every organisation. The controls below are the ones most applicable to software product security.

## Statement of Applicability

The Statement of Applicability (SoA) is a mandatory ISO 27001 document. It lists every Annex A control, states whether it applies, and justifies inclusion or exclusion.

For our purposes:

- **A.7 (Physical controls)** are largely delegated to cloud infrastructure providers. The SoA documents this delegation and the evidence chain (provider SOC 2 reports, ISO 27001 certificates).
- **A.6 (People controls)** apply but are organisational processes, not product-level technical controls.
- **A.5 and A.8** contain the controls most directly relevant to product security and are the focus of the mapping below.

## ISO 27017 additions

ISO 27017 extends ISO 27001 with controls specific to cloud service provision. These are relevant when the product operates as a cloud service or relies on cloud infrastructure.

| Area | What it adds | CSF subcategories |
|---|---|---|
| Shared responsibility | Explicit documentation of who owns what between provider and customer | <span style={{whiteSpace: 'nowrap'}}>GV.SC-05</span>, <span style={{whiteSpace: 'nowrap'}}>GV.RR-01</span> |
| Virtualisation security | Controls for hypervisor, container, and multi-tenant isolation | <span style={{whiteSpace: 'nowrap'}}>PR.PS-01</span> |
| Cloud service monitoring | Monitoring and logging requirements specific to cloud environments | <span style={{whiteSpace: 'nowrap'}}>DE.CM-01</span>, <span style={{whiteSpace: 'nowrap'}}>DE.CM-09</span> |
| Data location and portability | Controls for data residency, portability, and deletion assurance | <span style={{whiteSpace: 'nowrap'}}>PR.DS-01</span>, <span style={{whiteSpace: 'nowrap'}}>PR.DS-10</span> |
