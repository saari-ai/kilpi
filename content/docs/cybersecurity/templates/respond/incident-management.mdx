### Definition of Done

- [ ] Escalation matrix maps every severity level to a response urgency and notification path (RS.MA)
- [ ] Investigation and evidence preservation procedures documented (RS.AN)
- [ ] Internal and external notification requirements defined, including regulatory deadlines (RS.CO)
- [ ] Containment and eradication actions defined for each incident type in the threat register (RS.MI)
