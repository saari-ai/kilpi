The Discovery Brief is the primary deliverable of Product Discovery. It summarises the problem, evidence, insights, opportunity assessment, and recommendation in a single document. It is the artifact that gets handed off to [Product Validation](/docs/product-validation) or used to justify a pivot or kill decision.

See [Communicating Findings](/docs/product-discovery/synthesis/communicating-findings) for guidance on how to fill in each section and adapt the brief for different audiences.
