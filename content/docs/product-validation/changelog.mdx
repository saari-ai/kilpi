## 2026-02-27

- Initial release of the Product Validation methodology
- Five phases: Hypothesize, Design, Execute, Evaluate, Decide
- Traceability chain: Assumption (VA-) → Hypothesis (VH-) → Experiment (VE-) → Evidence (VV-) → Decision (VD-)
- Template set: Validation Plan, Experiment Card, Decision Record
- Technique catalog with 15 validation methods across 5 fidelity levels
- FocusNote worked example with three experiments and a Pivot decision
- Conventions page with ID schemes, confidence levels, risk levels, and assumption types
- Scope page defining boundaries and handoff contracts with Product Discovery and Build
