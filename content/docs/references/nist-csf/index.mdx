This section contains the raw NIST CSF 2.0 subcategory definitions and implementation examples, organized by function. This is reference data -- for our methodology on how to achieve these outcomes, see the category pages under each function.

## Functions

- **[Govern (GV)](./govern)** -- Organizational context, risk management strategy, roles and responsibilities, policy, oversight, and supply chain risk management.
- **[Identify (ID)](./identify)** -- Asset management, risk assessment, and improvement.
- **[Protect (PR)](./protect)** -- Identity management and access control, awareness and training, data security, platform security, and technology infrastructure resilience.
- **[Detect (DE)](./detect)** -- Continuous monitoring and adverse event analysis.
- **[Respond (RS)](./respond)** -- Incident management, analysis, reporting and communication, and mitigation.
- **[Recover (RC)](./recover)** -- Incident recovery plan execution and recovery communication.
